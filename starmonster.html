About

Star Monster is a multi-instrumentalist based out of Madison, Wisconsin. The project was launched in 2018 bridging the gap between Bass Music and Live Electronica while bringing a game changing take on visual experience. Star Monster has already developed significant traction in the bass music scene all over the US from small to large companies & communities such as FPC Live, Vital Events, Lex Martin presents, Camp Questionmark (Burning Man), Olympus Events, Ocular Presents, LoFreq Events & Records, Wormhole Entertainment, Spicy Bois and more.

He has played alongside top talent such as Flume, 12th Planet, Borgore, Rusko, Moody Good, Clozee, So Down, Minnesota, Zeke Beats, Boogie T, Champagne Drip, Space Laces, Must Die, Trampa, Dirt Monkey, Figure, G-Rex, and more.

His innovative and often mystical style of girthy bass music has fans shaking booty and spiritually centering all at once. During this Covid 19 pandemic he has been hard at work bringing live streams and new music to his fans.
