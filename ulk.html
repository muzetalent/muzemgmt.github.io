About

ULK, short for UnLucky Kid, is the alias for bass music producer Ross Rautmann from Appleton, Wisconsin. ULK quickly burst onto the scene with a shockingly long list of high-fidelity releases, most notably with the group of producers known as Spicy Bois, and looks to shake the foundation of bass music with an ever-evolving taste for the eccentric.
