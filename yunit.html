About

Dave Chapman, AKA YUNIT., is a Wisconsin native who has been keeping the underground riddim sound alive. YUNIT. has been quickly making a name for himself with significant releases on labels such as GRVDNCR, Fuckshit Records, Bewildered Productions, & many more! His unique sound has gained him support from artists such as Subtronics, HE$H, Bommer, and more. YUNIT. has been on a tear and has much more to come.
