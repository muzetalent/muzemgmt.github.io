About

Based out of Denver, Colorado, DJ and producer Cody Blair has made a splash as a hard hitting Riddim producer, going by the name Morf. Releasing songs on labels such as Buygore, The Riddim Network, Dirty Snatcha Promotions, Damaged Sounds and more, all while gaining the support of artists such as Megalodon, Whales, Jkyl x Hyde plus many others. Morf is known for his booming basslines and pristine style. Previously direct support for artists including Aweminus, MVRDA, Svdden Death and others, he has proven that he is a force to be reckoned with.
