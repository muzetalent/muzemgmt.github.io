About

Xotix is a bass music boy-band with a distinctive personality and a lot to say. This is not your average trio, as each individual has honed in a completely unique style over the years, that comes together in an explosive and synergetic dynamic. The group's attitude and charismatic approach to songwriting and performance goes for a textured and girthy sound that favors the human connection between the musicians and one that echoes humanities earliest and most primal experiences of being together.
