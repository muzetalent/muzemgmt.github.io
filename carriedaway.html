About

Born and raised in Colorado, DJ, producer and artist Cameron Love has begun to take the scene by storm with his new hybrid future-dubstep project, Carried Away. His two egos (White and Black) embody the difference between the two genres, clashing for power. Whether you’re looking for something melodic or something bass-heavy, you’ll find all that you are looking for and more with Carried Away.
